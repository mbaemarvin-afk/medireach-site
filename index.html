About MediReach Medical Clinic

MediReach Medical Clinic (M.E.D.I.R.E.A.C.H — Mobile-Enabled Digital Intelligence for Remote Engagement and AI-Powered Community Health) is a digital health-focused clinic in Chogoria, Kenya. We combine quality care with mobile-first tools such as WhatsApp engagement, digital follow-up, and data-informed prevention to improve patient experience and strengthen community health.

Mission

Improve access to care and strengthen prevention through practical digital health solutions.

Approach

Human-centered care supported by simple, low-bandwidth digital workflows.

Focus

Primary care, education, continuity, and responsible use of health data.

Services

Practical services designed for everyday health needs, supported by digital follow-up.

Consultation

Assessment, guidance, and referrals when needed.

First Aid & Wound Care

Basic wound management, dressings, and follow-up care.

Vitals & Screening

BP, temperature, BMI, and general health checks.

Health Education

Prevention guidance, lifestyle coaching, and community learning.

Chronic Care Follow-Up

Support for follow-ups and adherence reminders.

Referrals & Coordination

Connecting patients to higher-level care and follow-up tracking.

Digital Health Focus

WhatsApp engagement

Appointment coordination, reminders, follow-ups, and patient guidance via WhatsApp.

MediReach PHIN

PHIN (Predictive Health Intelligence Network) is a concept under development to support earlier, data-informed action using community signals and digital tools.
